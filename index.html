How This Works

This tool helps you build a custom sourdough recipe based on total flour weight and flour blend.

  • Flour Weight: Set how much flour you're using (e.g. 400g total).
  • Bread Flour Slider: Adjust to set how much is bread vs. whole wheat.
  • Protein %: These affect how much water your flour absorbs.
  • Hydration: The app recommends hydration based on your flour blend and protein level.
  • Starter: Assumes 20% of total flour weight. Can be customized in future versions.
  • Print Summary: Click to print your custom formula!
