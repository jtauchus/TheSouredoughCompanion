About This App

This tool helps you build a basic sourdough formula based on your flour blend and total flour weight.

Hydration is automatically calculated based on the protein content of your flours. Generally:

  • Higher protein flours absorb more water
  • Whole wheat tends to absorb more than white flour

Use the sliders and dropdowns to fine-tune the blend. The tool then displays grams of water and salt needed (salt = 2% of total flour).
