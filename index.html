Breadmaking Stages

  1. Feed Starter: Refresh starter 8–12 hours before use.
  2. Activate Starter: Use when doubled and bubbly.
  3. Make Dough: Mix flour, water, and starter. Add salt after autolyse.
  4. Build Dough Structure: Stretch and fold every 30–45 min.
  5. Bulk Rise: Let rise 3–6 hrs until ~doubled in size.
  6. Pre-shape: Rest dough 20–30 minutes after light shaping.
  7. Final Shape: Form dough into final shape for proofing.
  8. Proofing: Room temp (1–2 hrs) or cold proof overnight.
  9. Bake: Score and bake at 450–475°F until golden.
